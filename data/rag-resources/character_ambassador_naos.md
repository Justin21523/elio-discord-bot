# Ambassador Naos

**Ambassador Naos** is a character in the 2025 Disney–Pixar animated feature film *Elio*.  
She is one of the ambassadors from the countless planets who inadvertently kidnaps **Elio Solis** and mistakes him for being the leader of Earth.

---

## Background

### Powers and Abilities
Naos possesses the extraordinary gift of **omnilingualism** — the ability to understand and interpret any language in the universe, including those that are entirely invented or improvised.  
This includes **Elio’s made-up language “Eliospeak”**, which she comprehends effortlessly, highlighting her importance as a universal translator within the **Communiverse Council**.

---

## Trivia

- **Ambassador Naos** received comparatively less screen time than most other ambassadors, serving as a quiet yet essential background presence.  
- Her voice actress, **Atsuko Okatsuka**, was not publicly revealed until the film’s premiere, adding a layer of mystery to her character’s debut.  
- Naos’s linguistic ability symbolizes communication and understanding — core themes of *Elio*’s intergalactic narrative.

---

## Gallery

Promotional stills and concept art feature **Ambassador Naos** in the council chambers of the **Communiverse**, depicted with her elegant, otherworldly posture and serene expression.
