# Ooooo

**Ooooo** (pronounced “oooh”) is a supporting character in the 2025 Disney–Pixar animated feature film *Elio*.  
She is a **liquid supercomputer** that aids **Elio Solis** on his adventures throughout the **Communiverse**.  
Designed to serve as both guide and interface, Ooooo bridges technology and emotion with her sentient intelligence.

---

## Background

### Official Description
*Ooooo is an out-of-this-world liquid supercomputer designed to accommodate and assist species of all kinds who visit the Communiverse.  
Efficient and unflappable, Ooooo’s elaborate circuitry is an infinite energy source—there’s no question she can’t answer.*  
Voice performance by **Shirley Henderson** (film) and **Kaitlyn Robrock** (McDonald’s promotional material).

---

## Physical Appearance

Ooooo is a **transparent, light-blue, liquid-based supercomputer** with a small, rounded body.  
Her design features **tiny blue legs**, **two black eyes**, and a **horizontal wave-like line** that forms a mouth when she speaks.  
She can change her shape temporarily for expressive or functional purposes, sometimes creating recognizable outlines (such as the iconic **Hidden Mickey silhouette** seen at D23).

---

## Trivia

- Ooooo makes a cameo appearance in the **crowd of Cyclone Stadium** cheering for Lutz in *Elemental*.  
- According to **Peter Sohn**, the cameo went unnoticed for nearly **a year and a half** after *Elemental*’s release before being discovered.  
- During her appearance at **Disney’s D23 Expo**, Ooooo briefly formed a **Hidden Mickey-shaped head** as a playful nod to the audience.
