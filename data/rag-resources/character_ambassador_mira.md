# Ambassador Mira

**Ambassador Mira** is a character in the 2025 Disney–Pixar animated feature film *Elio*.  
She serves as the **leader and ambassador of a distant alien planet**, who—along with other representatives of the Communiverse—mistakenly identifies **Elio Solis** as the leader of Earth.

---

## Background

### Official Description
*Ambassador Mira is an empress from a distant alien planet.  
She is the most cunning of all the Ambassadors and, despite her sweet voice and adorable disposition, she will not hesitate to do whatever’s necessary to protect the Communiverse.*

Her demeanor reflects the balance between charm and shrewd leadership, establishing her as one of the most perceptive figures among the interplanetary ambassadors.

---

## Personality

Mira presents a gentle, graceful presence, characterized by her **soft-spoken nature** and **strategic intelligence**.  
Though she often appears calm and kind, she possesses **sharp diplomatic instincts** and a firm sense of responsibility.  
Her primary motivation is maintaining peace and order within the Communiverse, even when it requires making difficult or morally complex decisions.

---

## Physical Appearance

Mira’s design is inspired by ethereal, celestial motifs.  
She has a **large rounded head**, **big purple eyes**, and a **soft cloud-like body** resembling mist or vapor.  
Her form appears weightless—she floats elegantly, wearing what resembles a delicate **dress made of vapor or light**.  
This dreamlike appearance contrasts with her pragmatic and sometimes calculating personality.

---

## Trivia

- Mira shares her first name with **Mira Nova** from the Pixar animated series *Buzz Lightyear of Star Command*.  
- Her **voice is performed by Anissa Borrego**, who brings a calm yet commanding tone to her dialogue.  
- Mira’s design theme of clouds and light visually symbolizes clarity, serenity, and the dual nature of calmness concealing power.

---

## Gallery

Promotional artwork often places Mira near the central assembly chamber of the Communiverse, signifying her senior status among the ambassadors.
