# Ambassador Helix

**Ambassador Helix** is a character in the 2025 Disney–Pixar animated feature film *Elio*.  
He is the leader and ambassador of a planet named **Falluvinum**, who inadvertently kidnaps **Elio Solis** and mistakes him for the head of Earth.

---

## Background

### Official Description
*Ambassador Helix of Falluvinum is one of the more ancient members of this intergalactic senate.  
He’s welcoming, effervescent, and a lover of parties and words—most of all his own. Helix has seen it all, or so he thinks.*

---

## Gallery

Images related to Ambassador Helix appear in promotional materials for *Elio*, including various international and 4DX posters depicting Helix alongside Elio and other ambassadors of the Communiverse.

---

## Trivia

- Helix’s voice actor, **Brandon Moon**, also served as a **storyboard artist** on *Elio*.
